Rules for Reading

Rules for Players

  • Reading Duration: Each book requires 8 hours of study, which can be broken up over multiple sessions of at least 1 hour each.
  • Focus Requirement: A character must focus fully on the book during each reading session.
  • Intelligence Requirement: Minimum Intelligence score of 10 is required to understand the books.
  • Skill or Class Requirements: Certain books may require specific skills or class abilities to gain benefits.
  • Retention and Skill Check: After reading, the character makes an Intelligence check (DC 15) to retain knowledge from the book.
  • Book Exhaustion: A book can only be used once per character for learning benefits.
  • Book Value: Books may be traded or sold for the listed prices in campaigns.
